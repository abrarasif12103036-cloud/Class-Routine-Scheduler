   Make sure there are no merged cells

   Check that day names are spelled correctly
   Make sure days are in order: Mon, Tue, Wed, Thu, Fri, Sat, Sun

   If you have multiple sheets, put your schedule in the first sheet
   Remove any merged cells - they can cause parsing issues
